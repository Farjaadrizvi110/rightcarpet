About Right Carpets & Wood Floors

Our Story

For over two decades, Right Carpets & Wood Floors has been serving the Lancashire community with premium flooring solutions. What started as a small family business has grown into a trusted name in the region.

We pride ourselves on offering an extensive range of carpets, laminates, vinyls, and wood flooring options to suit every taste and budget. Our commitment to quality products and exceptional customer service has made us the go-to choice for homeowners across the area.
